# Superstore Sales Dashboard – Power BI

## Objective
Developed an interactive Power BI dashboard to analyze sales performance, profitability, customer segments, shipping trends, and regional performance. The dashboard transforms raw sales data into actionable insights, enabling stakeholders to make informed, data-driven business decisions.

## Key Insights
- Office Supplies emerged as the highest-selling category (≈0.15M), followed by Furniture and Technology.
- The Consumer segment contributed nearly 48% of total sales, making it the largest revenue-generating customer group.
- Standard Class was the most preferred shipping mode, while premium shipping options remained underutilized, indicating potential for growth.
- Monthly profit analysis showed noticeable declines during March and April, highlighting periods that may require operational and pricing improvements.
- California, New York, and Texas consistently ranked as the top-performing states in terms of sales.
- Digital payment methods (Cards and Online Payments) accounted for approximately 80% of all transactions, reflecting a strong customer preference for cashless payments.

## Business Impact
- Identified opportunities to increase revenue by promoting premium shipping services and expanding focus on the Corporate customer segment.
- Revealed high-performing regions to support targeted marketing campaigns and better resource allocation.
- Highlighted seasonal profit fluctuations, helping identify areas for cost optimization and improved business planning.
- Delivered an interactive dashboard that enables stakeholders to monitor key performance indicators (KPIs) and make faster, data-driven decisions.
